WHO WE ARE

We`re a construction company which prides itself in redefining building innovation on a national scale

With a focus on transforming spaces, Trendy Builds excels in renovating residential and commercial properties. Trendy Builds provides innovative remodeling solutions, tailored to meet the unique needs and preference of clients. From designing beautiful outdoor spaces to executing flawless landscaping projects, Trendy Builds enhances aesthetics and functionality of properties.

Trendy Builds is dedicated to delivering exceptional construction services, exceeding client expectations, and creating sustainable and inspiring spaces. SERVICE

Construction

We construct domestic, commercial, and industrial properties from the ground up, ensuring the utmost quality and efficiency. Our construction services also includes demolishing of old buildings, planning and construction of new buildings.

Paving

We provide professional paving and repair services, including the use of fresh asphalt and proper compaction with a roller to make the surface appear new. Our paving services also include, Concrete, Brick, Flagstone, Marble, Travertine, Porcelain, Cobblestone paving.

Plumbing

We Install, repair, and maintain pipes, valves, fittings, drainage systems, and fixtures in both commercial and residential structures. Trendy Builds plumbing experts cut, assemble and install pipes and tubes with attention to existing infrastructure.

Electric Installations

We also specialize in designing, installing, and maintaining electrical systems. Whether it`s high-voltage transmission or low-voltage lighting, Trendy Builds Electricians ensure these systems work in a safe, effective and environmentally conscious way.

Remodeling

Our experienced professionals also install high-quality products across an entire line of full-service home and commercial remodeling solutions. From replacing windows and doors to kitchen and bathroom remodeling to expanding and upgrading roofing and office spaces.

Rubble Removal

Trendy Builds makes it convenient for you to handle large and messy tasks such as picking up the rubble that builders left behind and transporting them to a landfill or recycling centre. You won`t need to shovel the debris yourself or drive to the dump.
